# Pipes
<ItemGrid>
 <ItemIcon id="meeptech:item_pipe" /> <ItemIcon id="meeptech:fluid_pipe" /> <ItemIcon id="meeptech:wrench" />
</ItemGrid>
Pipes are a convenient way to transfer items and fluids. Item and fluid pipes cost about 1 bronze each. You can connect pipes of the same type by placing a pipe against another one. Wrenches can also connect or disconnect pipes.
## Extractors and Inserters
<ItemGrid>
 <ItemIcon id="meeptech:item_extractor" /> <ItemIcon id="meeptech:item_inserter" /> <ItemIcon id="meeptech:fluid_extractor" /> <ItemIcon id="meeptech:fluid_inserter" />
</ItemGrid>
Pipes do not directly have a limit on what can go through them or on their throughput (transfer rate). However, they need extractors and inserters in order to interface with inventories or tanks.
Extractors are the inputs into a pipe network, and have limits on their throughput.
Inserters are the outputs of a pipe network, and do not have limits on their throughput.
