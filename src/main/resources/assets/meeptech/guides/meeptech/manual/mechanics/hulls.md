# Machine Hulls
<ItemImage id="meeptech:iron_hull" />
Hulls are the basis of a machine. Each hull has a [Machine Core](../machine_cores.md) module slot. Once a hull is filled with a complete machine core, it will become functional.
## Notes
- Filled or partially filled hulls will drop with their modules inside of them when broken. Machines with items inside of them will drop those items when broken.
- Machines support automation, such as through hoppers.
