# Material Workstation
<ItemImage id="meeptech:material_workstation" />
The material workstation is used in the early game to turn items into different [Forms](forms.md) of the same [Material](materials.md). For example, it can turn an iron ingot into an iron plate.
## Crafting
First, make a hammer, which is crafted in this format:
<RecipeFor id="meeptech:stone_hammer" />
Then, make the workstation itself. The hammer is not consumed but it takes one damage.
<RecipeFor id="meeptech:material_workstation" />
## Usage
To use the material workstation, insert the material into the first slot, and the hammer into the hammer slot. It'll show you all forms that you can craft with the item amount, similar to a stonecutter. Then simply click on the form you want to get, and it'll perform that recipe, putting the item in the output slot. 

The hammer will take one damage for each recipe. You can also shift click to make as much as possible.
