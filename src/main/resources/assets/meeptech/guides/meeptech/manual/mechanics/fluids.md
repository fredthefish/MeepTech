# Fluids
You'll be working with fluids a lot. 
## Tanks
<ItemImage id="meeptech:fluid_tank" />
For convenient fluid storage, there is the fluid tank. It can store up to eight buckets of one fluid, and keeps the fluid inside of it when broken.
## Cells
<ItemImage id="meeptech:fluid_cell" />
For convenient fluid transfer, there is the fluid cell. Unlike buckets, it cannot get or place fluids from the environment, but it can stack up to 64 with fluid cells of the same type and amount, and it can store portions of a bucket. You can also shift click in the GUI of fluid tanks or fluid slots to try to take out as much fluid as possible into the stack, and you can right click to put fluid from the stack into the tank.
