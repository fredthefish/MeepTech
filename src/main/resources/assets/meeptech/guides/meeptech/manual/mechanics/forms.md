# Material Forms
Each material has different forms, although not all materials will necessarily have all forms. Recipes for forms are always similar across materials. Some material forms can be used as components for modules.
## Ingots
<ItemImage id="minecraft:iron_ingot" />
Ingots are the base form of a material.
## Plates
<ItemImage id="meeptech:iron_plate" />
Plates are made from ingots (1 ingot = 1 plate).
## Boxes
<ItemImage id="meeptech:iron_box" />
Boxes are made from plates (6 plates = 1 box).
## Hulls
<ItemImage id="meeptech:iron_hull" />
Hulls are made from plates (8 plates = 1 hull). For more information on hulls, see [Hulls](hulls.md).
